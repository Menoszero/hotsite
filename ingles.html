The event

The International Congress of Children with Complex Health Conditions will be held from April 24th to 28th, 2023.

We will discuss recent world advances in the knowledge and application of technologies for comprehensive and humanized care for children with complex health conditions.

The panels bring relevant scientific themes that translate into advanced technologies for diagnosis, treatment and cure.

We will raise the discussion on technologies, which are related to advances in comprehensive and humanized care for children, enabling innovations in care.

Courses and short courses

SUBMIT YOUR abstract

Electronic poster

In a space specially dedicated to Electronic poster, the selected authors will present their scientific work to the evaluators of the International Congress of Children with Complex Health Conditions at the end of each day's schedule. Virtually, the scientific works can be accessed and re-accessed at any time by the participants, combined with a recorded audio of the presentation.

Know More

Oral presentation

The oral presentations of the scientific works submitted and approved will be made in the rooms of the International Congress of Children with Complex Health Conditions, every day, with a previously scheduled time. Virtually, the presentations will be broadcast live in our metaverse.

BE A SPONSOR

At the HCB Congress, we will raise important discussions about advances in technologies that use comprehensive care and humanization in the treatment of children, enabling innovations in their care.

In addition, the event will feature symposiums of extreme relevance for specialized pediatrics, exhibitions of new technologies, equipment and products, as well as debates on specialized content, with courses and short courses throughout its duration.
